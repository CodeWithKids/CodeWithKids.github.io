STEM Clubs

The after-school STEM club is designed for kids and takes place outside regular school hours, focusing on exploring STEM concepts and skills. These clubs typically meet in schools and provide engaging, hands-on activities to help children deepen their interest and knowledge in STEM subjects.

STEM Holiday Bootcamps

The Holiday STEM Bootcamps for Kids is a fun and educational program held during school breaks. It provides students with hands-on experiences in STEM fields, allowing them to engage in interactive activities related to science, technology, engineering, and mathematics. These bootcamps are designed to inspire and develop key skills such as problem-solving, creativity, and critical thinking.

Space Tech

The Space Tech program introduces students to the exciting world of space technology and its applications. Learners explore cutting-edge topics like satellite design, space missions, and robotics used in space exploration. Through hands-on activities and real-world simulations, this program aims to equip learners with the skills and knowledge necessary for future careers in space science and technology.
